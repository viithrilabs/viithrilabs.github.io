App Development

Native and cross-platform apps for iOS and Android. Performance-first development that results in apps users genuinely enjoy using — and keep coming back to.

What we do

We build mobile applications from scratch — native iOS in Swift, native Android in Kotlin, and cross-platform with React Native or Flutter for projects where a unified codebase makes sense. Every app we deliver is tested across real devices, optimised for performance, and designed with the end user in mind from day one.

We also handle App Store and Google Play submissions, analytics integration, and ongoing maintenance after launch.

Our process
01
Discovery and Scoping
We map out the full feature set, user flows, platform requirements, and technical architecture before a line of code is written.
02
Design and Prototype
High-fidelity Figma designs and interactive prototypes. You review and approve screens before we move into development.
03
Development Sprints
Two-week sprints with testable builds at the end of each. You always have visibility into what is being built and when.
04
QA and Device Testing
Testing across a real device matrix — multiple screen sizes, OS versions, and network conditions. No simulator shortcuts.
05
Store Submission
App Store and Play Store submission including metadata, screenshots, privacy disclosures, and review handling.
06
Post-Launch Support
Bug fixes, OS compatibility updates, and feature iterations. We stay on to ensure the app succeeds after launch day.
