What we do

ViiThri Labs builds games across platforms — mobile hyper-casual, narrative PC titles, and immersive VR/AR experiences. We handle the full lifecycle: design, development, QA, store submission, and post-launch support. Every game we ship is built with a player-first mentality and performance-optimised code.

Whether you have a polished concept or just a rough idea, we work from where you are to where you need to be.

Our process
01
Discovery and Concept
We deep-dive into your vision — target platform, audience, genre, mechanics, and art direction. We align on scope and produce a Game Design Document before any code is written.
02
Prototype
A playable prototype in 2–4 weeks. Core loop, basic physics, rough visuals. This is where we validate the idea is actually fun before scaling the production.
03
Alpha Development
Full feature development — all mechanics, levels, systems, and art. Regular builds for internal testing and stakeholder review throughout the sprint cycle.
04
Beta and QA
Comprehensive testing across devices, OS versions, and edge cases. Performance profiling and optimisation. Closed and open beta with real player feedback loops.
05
Launch
Store submission with metadata, screenshots, trailers, and localisation. We handle the submission process end-to-end and coordinate the release day.
06
Live Operations
Post-launch updates, seasonal content, analytics monitoring, and player feedback loops. We keep the game growing long after it ships.
