ViiThri's Oven

A full eCommerce experience built for a Chennai-based home bakery. Designed and developed end-to-end — from brand identity to order fulfilment, serving real customers every week.

The brief

ViiThri's Oven is a homemade bakery specialising in custom cakes, cookies, and festive specials. The client needed a complete online presence — a brand identity that felt warm and artisanal, and an eCommerce site that made ordering genuinely easy for customers who were previously ordering only through social media DMs.

The goal was to move the entire order and payment process online, reduce back-and-forth communication, and give the bakery a professional home on the web.

What we built
Product Catalogue
Browseable catalogue with photography-first layout, filtering by category and occasion
Custom Order Form
Guided order flow for custom cakes — flavour, size, design, delivery date
Online Payments
UPI, card, and net banking via Razorpay integration with order confirmation
Order Management
Admin dashboard for the baker to manage incoming orders, statuses, and dates
Email Notifications
Automated confirmation and status update emails for every order milestone
Mobile-First Design
Fully responsive — the majority of customers order from mobile devices
Tech stack
HTML / CSS / JavaScript Razorpay Formspree GitHub Pages Custom domain Email automation
Design approach

The visual identity uses a warm cream and burnt orange palette — intentionally different from the indigo-dark studio brand. Playfair Display for headlines gives an artisanal, premium bakery feel. Photography is the hero on every product page. The entire experience is designed to make you hungry before you even get to checkout.
